About Our Todo List App

Welcome to our todo list app!

Our app is a simple and easy-to-use tool that helps you keep track of your tasks and stay organized. With our app, you can create a list of tasks, set deadlines, and mark tasks as completed when you're done.

Features

  • Add tasks to your todo list with just a few clicks
  • Mark tasks as completed and move them to your completed list
  • Filter tasks by priority or deadline
  • Sync your todo list across all your devices

Technology

Our app is built using modern web technologies, such as Django and Bootstrap, to provide a fast and responsive user experience.
